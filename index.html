About me

I am a third year mathematics PhD student at Stanford University, advised by Amir Dembo.

I am broadly interested in probability theory and its applications to mathematical/statistical physics. Some particular topics of interest include Gibbs measures, line ensembles, spin systems, and random polymers.

Before coming to Stanford, I was an undergraduate at Columbia University, where I worked with Ivan Corwin and Evgeni Dimitrov.
